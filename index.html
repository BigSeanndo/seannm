About Me

Currently in my second year at Edinburgh Napier University, I am pursuing a degree in cyber-security. This field fascinates me with its constant evolution and significant role in protecting our digital world. My interests extend beyond academics. I practice mixed martial arts, a discipline that enhances both my physical strength and mental resilience. Additionally, playing the drums offers me a creative escape, balancing the demands of my studies. I also enjoy hiking, finding peace and rejuvenation in nature's landscapes. This hobby helps me unwind and gain perspective. Overall, my journey as a cyber-security student is shaped by a combination of academic dedication, physical activity, musical passion, and a love for the outdoors. Each of these elements plays a vital role in my personal and professional growth.
